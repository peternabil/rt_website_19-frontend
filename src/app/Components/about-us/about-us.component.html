About Us

WHO WE ARE ?

“ASU Racing Team” is a student organization founded in 2010, in the faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University. It consists of a group of dedicated undergraduate engineering students aiming to revive the research in Egypt especially in the automotive technology, not only by theorizing but also by apply the theory in real life by building cars and robots. Academically supervised by a group of doctors and TAs from the faculty of Engineering, Ain Shams University.

WHAT WE DO ?

We mainly participate in 3 major international competitions using cars, prototypes and robots that we design and manufacture inside faculty premises.

OUR MISSION

To establish an advanced Egyptian research entity with divisions concerned with Automotive Engineering and Mobile Robotics. - With 90% of our car MADE IN EGYPT, designed and manufactured by undergraduate engineering students, we are trying to deliver a message that Egyptian youth has the capabilities and creativity required to lead and innovate! All we need is a little push. (And we may add a foot pressing the gas paddle after this last line. However, you may find it cheesy)

OUR VISION

Our main vision to keep up with the fast growing innovation in automotive technology worldwide by: researching, learning and applying this knowledge on ground to revive the automotive industry in Egypt.

WHY WE ARE UNIQUE FROM ANOTHER STUDENT ACTIVITES

Alongside the academic value we try to offer our engineering students, ASU racing Team is an organization that has a physical product that the students make from A to Z.
